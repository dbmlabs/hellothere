Nutanix

Customers are telling me Nutanix is easier to operate and more mature with #data-locality, deduplication, compression, and replication. One use case is Nutanix with Acropolis to displace vSphere for running Citrix. A second use case is Nutanix with #Acropolis for test and dev servers. Nutanix has a converter to move the VMs to vSphere in production.
#vSphere #Citrix #Nutanix

Citrix

Actively working a campaign at CHI vs. Citrix CHI is looking for us to embrace and potentially enhance the legacy Citrix environments. We are beginning to gather requirements for a POC that will be hosted within St. Lukes Hospital in Houston TX. Have had good collaborative meetings thus far with the project manager however we are early in the conversation.
#citrix

VMware Integrated OpenStack at FedEx

#FedEx is currently in a POC evaluating #VIO. The #FedEx team met with @SanthoshSundararaman and @MarcosHernandez during a VBC at VMworld and found that the next release of #VIO 2.0 will solve several things that they are looking forward to, Neutron service has a known bug, if the controller node goes down the services do not restart automatically, they have to be gracefully restarted from the GUI. Load balancing as a service is coming as well as the ability to create branding with in the GUI.
#openstack #fedex

Customer First Focus on BDE

McKesson had an urgent needs for PSO assistance on their #BDE deployment and the CustomerFirst mentality kicked in across the VMware team to enable Faisal from GSS to fulfill the need with his historic knowledge of McKesson. We were able to keep BDE in the Hadoop Cloud deployment, move McKesson close to production deployment, and also educated some of the Hadoop consultants from CenturyLInk and increase their BDE knowledge and comfort. Huge thank you to Faisal, his GSS management, and his new Take3 Manager in support of McKesson!
#BDE #GSS #Take3

vRealize Operations 6

VMware PSO delivered a clean-build vROps 6 implementation for EMC IT Command Center. The platform has already built credibility, especially when VMware PSO Consultant Craig Riesinger used vROps to document how one the vSphere clusters was overloaded and performed a “What if” analysis to document a resolution.
#vROps6 #PSO

BofA going homegrown via OpenStack

Banks current go forward direction is to phase out the remaining integration points of VCAC and implement their own homegrown automation software via openstack. This will enable them not to have any vendor lock-in in the management of both the VMware hypervisor platform and/or KVM. They are to go live on Oct 15th. They still will have the availability of VRA with the pending recent CloudSuite purchase should they choose to implement later. No interest in VIO at the moment
#vRA #openstack #VIO
